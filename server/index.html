Welcome to my shrine dedicated to Natsume Takashi!!



ABOUT THE BOOK

Natsume's Book of Friends (Natsume Yuujinchou) is a beautiful and emotional story about a boy named Takashi Natsume who can see spirits called youkai.

He inherits a mysterious book from his grandmother that contains the names of powerful spirits. Now he must return their names and protect himself from dangerous youkai!



CHARACTERS
  • Takashi Natsume
  • Nyanko-sensei (Madara)
  • Reiko Natsume
  • Various Youkai Friends




Why I Love This Series

This story makes me cry EVERY TIME!!! It teaches kindness, loneliness, friendship, and understanding. Nyanko-sensei is the BEST cat spirit ever!!!!
